Tofu Stir-Fry

Description of the recipe

Tofu is an incredible source of protein and mixing it in a stir fry is a quick and easy choice. My love for this recipe came from a restaurant, which served tofu stir fry and I instantly fell in love with it. The recipe is described as nutritious, filling and flavourful, which counts towards your five a day. Also, did I mention this recipe is vegan?

Ingredients

  • 1 can of light coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 2x tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2x tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1x tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1x teaspoon of Sriracha sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground chili pepper
  • 1x tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2x carrots, diced
  • 1x red bell pepper, diced
  • 1x package firm tofu, drained and cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 4x garlic cloves, minced
  • 2x tablespoons of minced fresh ginger
  • 4x cups of baby spinach
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of cooked brown rice

Steps

  1. Whisk coconut milk, peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, Sriracha sauce, and ground chili pepper together in a bowl until a smooth sauce forms.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add carrots and bell pepper; sauté until just tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Add tofu and sauté until lightly browned, about 4 minutes per side. Add garlic and ginger; cook and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Pour sauce into the skillet and stir to coat tofu and vegetables. Cook until flavors combine, about 5 minutes. Reduce the heat to low, then stir in spinach, 1 cup at a time, until wilted. Serve over brown rice.
